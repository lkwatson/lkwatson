Basics

I'm fluent in the best practices of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I can implement advanced but eloquent code that's reusable, DRY, and well documented.

Complementary to these languages, I have a comprehensive understanding of client-server communication, particularly the details of communication over HTTP as well as Sockets.

I'm knowledgeable in each step from developing to deploying a site, including some often neglected parts like setting up correct DNS settings, declaring canonical URLs, sitemaps, robots.txt, and more.

Frontend

Frontend development is thrilling. I get to create something that anyone in the world can visit and interact with. I'm driven to make sites that are responsive and accessible to everyone.
