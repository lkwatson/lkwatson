Who I am.

I'm a student seeking to learn about the physical world and how we can apply groundbreaking research into our everyday lives. I'm pursuing a double degree in Mechanical Engineering and Physics, and have specifically focused my course work in acoustics, robotics, and controls.

Interests.

Chiefly, I'm interested in physical design and software that controls hardware at a high level (i.e. robotics, controls).

I enjoy working on various personal projects in IoT, machine vision, 3D printing, robotics, and more.

In terms of research, I'm most interested in acoustics and robotics (and perhaps their intersection).

I also co-produce a podcast, play the clarinet, and enjoy photography (especially film).
