Who I am.

I'm a university student seeking to both learn more about the physical world, and to apply emerging technologies and research to our everyday lives. I'm pursuing a double degree in Mechanical Engineering and Physics. Specifically, I'm focusing my course work in fields I'd like to work in: acoustics and quantum computation.

Interests.

I have a range of interests. I enjoy creating web and mobile apps in my spare time, along with some projects in IoT and lower-level hardware coding. As well, I spend a good deal of time designing and machining parts. Notably, I'm invested in 3D printing; I've built two 3D printers so far, and am an active member in the 3D printing community.

I also co-produce a podcast, play the clarinet, and photograph the world around me.
