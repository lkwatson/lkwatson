Who I am.

I'm a university student seeking to learn more about the physical world, and apply emerging technologies and research into to our everyday lives. I'm pursuing a double degree in Mechanical Engineering and Physics. Specifically, I'm focusing my course work in fields I'd like to work in: acoustics, quantum computation, and control theory.

Interests.

Chiefly, I'm interested in physical design, and software that controls hardware at a high level (i.e. robotics, controls).

In my spare time, I enjoy creating web and mobile apps, along with projects in IoT and low level hardware programming. As well, I enjoy designing and machining parts. Particularly, I'm invested in 3D printing; I've built two 3D printers so far, and am an active member in the 3D printing community.

I also co-produce a podcast, play the clarinet, and photograph the world around me.
